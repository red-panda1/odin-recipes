Lentil Soup-John Belushi

Story

The Rainbow Bar and Grill in L.A. was known for its lentil soup.

A very drunk John Belushi stopped in there on the night of March 5, 1982.

Belushi scarfed down a bowl of the lentil soup in the Rainbow's kitchen, then returned to his bungalow at Chateau Marmont.

That night, Belushi's girlfriend injected the 33-year-old comedian with what turned out to be a lethal dose of heroin and cocaine.

Ingredients

  • 7 cups water
  • 1 pound cooked, smoked lean ham, cut into chunks
  • 1 can low-sodium diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1â€‰3/4 cups dried lentils, rinsed
  • 8 ounces chopped onion
  • 6 ounces sliced carrots
  • 6 ounces chopped celery
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf

Steps

  1. Combine water, ham, tomatoes, lentils, onion, carrots, celery, garlic, pepper, salt, thyme, and bay leaf in a slow cooker.
  2. Cover and cook on LOW until lentils and vegetables are as soft as you like, 6 to 8 hours. Remove bay leaf before serving.
