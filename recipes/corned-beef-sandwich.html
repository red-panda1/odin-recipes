Corned Beef Sandwich-John Lennon

Story

During the afternoon of December 8, 1980, Lennon ate a corned beef sandwich before going to a New York recording studio to work on one of Yoko Ono's new singles.

At around 10:30 p.m., having just received the happy news that their album, Double Fantasy, had gone platinum, they decided to quit working for the night.

Upon returning to their Manhattan apartment a deranged fan shot and killed Lennon.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup Russian salad dressing
  • 8 slices Jewish rye bread
  • 1 pound thin-sliced corned beef
  • 1/2 pound prepared coleslaw

Steps

  1. Spread about 1/4 of the Russian salad dressing onto one side of each of four bread slices.
  2. Top each with 4 ounces corned beef and 2 ounces coleslaw.
  3. Finish sandwich construction with remaining bread slices.
  4. Halve sandwiches diagonally before serving.
